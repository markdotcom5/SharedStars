About SharedStars

Making Space Accessible for Everyone

Our Mission

Our mission is to democratize space travel by equipping individuals with the knowledge, certifications, AR/VR experiences, classroom training, and our comprehensive 10-module program. Through leaderboards that foster friendly competition and immersive experiences, SharedStars prepares aspiring space travelers to confidently step into the final frontier.

SharedStars transforms space travel dreams into reality by creating an inclusive and vibrant community of Subscribing Space Travelers (SSTs) who are united in shaping the future of space exploration.

With over 1.17 billion space enthusiasts worldwide, we aim to bridge the gap between aspiration and accessibility, providing tools, resources, and programs that prepare our members for the opportunities awaiting beyond Earth. A certification from SharedStars serves as your ticket to participate in the fast-growing space travel industry, setting a benchmark much like OSHA and ISO standards in occupational and aerospace industries.

Our Vision

To create a world where space travel is inclusive and attainable for all, fostering a global community united by the shared aspiration to explore beyond Earth.

How SharedStars Was Born

At one of the lowest points in my life, I grappled with a profound sense of despair—not just from personal challenges but also from a dream that felt impossibly out of reach. Space travel, with its astronomical costs ranging from hundreds of thousands to millions of dollars, seemed like a privilege reserved for only the wealthiest.

During this time, I discovered The Three-Body Problem trilogy by Liu Cixin. Its exploration of humanity’s resilience, ingenuity, and collective drive to overcome impossible odds ignited a spark within me. The story reminded me that innovation and collective effort can achieve what once seemed unattainable.

I realized space travel doesn’t have to remain an exclusive pursuit for the elite. From that moment of inspiration, SharedStars was born. It’s not just a platform; it’s a movement. SharedStars is dedicated to democratizing space travel by providing education, community, and opportunities for anyone willing to dream big and prepare for the next frontier.
